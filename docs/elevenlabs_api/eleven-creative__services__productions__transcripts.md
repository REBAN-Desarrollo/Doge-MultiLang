# Transcripts - ElevenLabs Productions

ElevenLabs offers human-edited transcripts in two formats:

**Non-Verbatim ("Clean")** - Default option for most use cases like podcasts and marketing. It "removes filler words, stutters, audio event tags for smoother reading."

**Verbatim** - For legal and research purposes, designed to "capture *exactly* what is said, including all filler words, stutters and audio event tags."

## Ordering Process

Users can order transcripts through:
- The Productions page in their ElevenLabs account
- The Speech to Text order dialog
- By requesting human review on existing transcripts

## Delivery & Pricing

The service aims to "deliver all transcripts **within 48 hours.**" Pricing varies based on language, transcription type, and content complexity, with custom quotes available through their sales team.

## Key Differences

Non-verbatim removes informal contractions, repetitions, and environmental sounds unless meaningful. Verbatim preserves everything audible, including stutters marked with hyphens, false starts with double hyphens, and all non-verbal human sounds.

Both formats correct mispronounced words and fully transcribe swear words.
