# Billing

> Manage your subscription, view pricing plans, and understand how credit rollover works.

## Key Navigation

- Pricing information: elevenlabs.io/pricing
- Subscription details: elevenlabs.io/app/subscription

## Subscription Overview

New users automatically receive free tier access. The platform offers six public subscription tiers: Free, Starter, Creator, Pro, Scale, and Business, plus a customizable Enterprise option.

## Plan Features

Voice cloning capabilities begin at the Starter tier. The free plan includes three custom voices created via the Voice Design tool or sourced from the Voice Library (excluding paid-tier-restricted voices).

## Credit Management

Unused credits carry forward monthly up to a two-month maximum, provided the subscription remains active without downgrades or cancellations. Upgrading transfers unused quota from previous plans.

## Downgrade Policy

Subscription downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle, preserving the current month's quota.

## Licensing Rights

Paid plan users receive commercial usage rights for generated content. Free plan users may use content non-commercially with attribution, as outlined in the Terms of Service and Help Center documentation.
