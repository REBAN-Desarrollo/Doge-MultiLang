# Dubbing Services by ElevenLabs Productions

ElevenLabs offers human-edited dubbing services to localize video content across languages. Users submit their video file, source language, and target language through the Productions platform to receive a production-ready dubbed version.

## Process Overview

The service follows a structured workflow:

1. **Order submission** via the Productions dashboard with video file (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV), source, and target language
2. **Processing** using AI with human oversight for transcription, translation, voice generation, and synchronization
3. **Delivery** in multiple formats including MP4, audio files, captions, and timeline data

## Quality Workflow

The team handles transcription, translation with native speaker review, voice selection, pacing adjustments, and quality control checks to ensure natural delivery.

## Key Details

- **Pricing**: Custom quotes based on language pairs, content type, and services
- **Turnaround**: Within 7 business days standard; faster options available for enterprise clients
- **Support**: Feedback mechanism available; edits possible via Dubbing Studio
- **Limitations**: Lip sync matching is attempted but not guaranteed
