# Subtitles Service Overview

ElevenLabs Productions offers human-edited subtitles and captions. Here are the key details:

## Key Differences

**Captions** transcribe dialogue and include non-speech sounds and speaker identification by default. They're intended for deaf/hard of hearing audiences and same-language viewers.

**Subtitles** translate dialogue for audiences unfamiliar with the source language. Non-speech elements and speaker IDs aren't included unless requested.

## Process

Users order subtitles through their ElevenLabs Productions account, receive email notification when ready, and export files in SRT format.

## Delivery & Pricing

Standard turnaround is "within 48-72 hours." Pricing varies based on languages, content type, and services. Enterprise clients needing faster delivery should contact productions@elevenlabs.io.

## Notable Limitations

The platform currently doesn't allow post-delivery edits directly within the system, though users can modify exported files externally. Feedback on completed projects can be submitted through the three-dot menu option.
